Sentiment analysis about Contact tracing apps

A study to understand the sentiments and opinons of the contact tracing apps

The following analysis was performed using around 430K tweets collected from July 2020 to February 2021. In order to extract opinions and sentiments pretrained BERT model have been used for each main langueges (English, Italian, French, German and Spanish) Around half tweets from this datasets not contain any sentiment or opinions and these are classified as neutral (224143 tweets). The rest of the tweets, three-quarters are negative and one-quarter positive.

The stacked barchar represents the tweets collected about the european contact tracing apps and classified as neutral, positive and negative.

Combining sentiment analysis and geo-extraction

The following maps show the geographical distribution of the positive and negative tweets. The datasets created is composed by the intersection of the tweets with opinions and place names. The places names have been extracted using nlp techniques plus the database geonames for the translation from extracted place names to coordinates.
